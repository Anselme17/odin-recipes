Raclette

Description

A dish of pure gastronomy where you enjoy both of these two monsters that are cheese and potatoes.

Ingredients :

  • Cheese from your choice
  • Onion and garlic
  • Jamon
  • Potatoes
  • Salt

To successfully complete this recipe, you will need to carefully follow each of the following steps, making sure to respect the order and quantities prescribed:

  1. Boil your potatoes
  2. Prepare the plates of jamon
  3. Install the machine
  4. Put your cheese under the resistance
  5. Wait until it's hot
