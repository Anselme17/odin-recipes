Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients :

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato product
  • Sugar
  • Spices

To successfully complete this recipe, you will need to carefully follow each of the following steps, making sure to respect the order and quantities prescribed:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta Mixture
  4. Do the layers of the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
