Poulet Pates

Description

It is a classic that never gets old for all my poor students

Ingredients :

  • Noodles
  • Chicken
  • Cream
  • Parmesan
  • cheese

To successfully complete this recipe, you will need to carefully follow each of the following steps, making sure to respect the order and quantities prescribed:

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Boil your noodles
  3. Wait for 8-10 min
  4. Fry your chicken
  5. Put all of it in your plate
